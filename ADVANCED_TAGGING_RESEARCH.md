## üèÜ **Best Practices from Industry Leaders**

### **1. RFM Segmentation (Proven Model)**

**Standard RFM Scoring:**
```
Recency Score (1-5):
5 = 0-30 days (Active)
4 = 31-60 days (Recent)
3 = 61-90 days (Moderate)
2 = 91-180 days (At-Risk)
1 = 180+ days (Churned)

Frequency Score (1-5):
5 = 10+ orders (Loyal)
4 = 7-9 orders (Frequent)
3 = 4-6 orders (Regular)
2 = 2-3 orders (Occasional)
1 = 1 order (One-time)

Monetary Score (1-5):
5 = Top 20% spenders (VIP)
4 = 20-40% (High Value)
3 = 40-60% (Medium Value)
2 = 60-80% (Low Value)
1 = Bottom 20% (Minimal)
```

**11 Key Customer Segments:**
1. **Champions (555)** - Best customers, highest value
2. **Loyal (X5X)** - Frequent buyers, any recency
3. **Potential Loyalist (45X)** - Recent, frequent, growing
4. **New Customers (5X1)** - Recent first purchase
5. **Promising (4X1-2)** - Recent, good spend, low frequency
6. **Need Attention (3XX)** - Above average but declining
7. **About to Sleep (2XX)** - Below average, losing them
8. **At Risk (X2X)** - Low recency, were good customers
9. **Cannot Lose (1X5)** - High value but inactive
10. **Hibernating (1X1-2)** - Low across all metrics
11. **Lost (111)** - Completely churned
